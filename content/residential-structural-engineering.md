## Residential Services

### Deck Permits
Stamped structural drawings for new decks and deck replacements, including footing sizes, post and beam framing, ledger connections, and guardrail attachment details. We provide exactly what the building department requires.

### Home Additions
Structural design for room additions, second-story additions, and bump-outs. Includes new foundation design or existing foundation evaluation, floor and roof framing, and any required lateral resisting elements.

### Garage Conversions and ADUs
Structural review and drawings for converting attached garages to living space, including floor upgrade evaluation, wall reinforcement, and beam designs for garage door opening modifications.

### Vaulted Ceiling Framing
Removing a ceiling to create vaulted space often affects structural elements. We evaluate the existing framing, design replacement ridge beams or similar, and provide drawings for the permit.

### Remodel Structural Sign-Off
Opening up walls, removing load-bearing elements, and adding skylights all require structural evaluation. We review your plans, design the supporting structure, and provide stamped drawings.

### Structural Letters for Building Permits
Some jurisdictions require a structural engineer letter for permit applications even for smaller projects. We provide concise, professional letters that satisfy building department requirements.

### Foundation Evaluation
Cracked foundations, differential settlement, bowing basement walls, and similar concerns. We inspect, evaluate, and provide a written report with our findings and recommendations.

## Colorado Springs Building Requirements

The Pikes Peak Regional Building Department requires stamped structural drawings for most structural work, including decks over a certain height, additions, and load-bearing wall modifications. Colorado's mountain climate means higher wind and snow loads than many other states — a generic internet plan is rarely adequate. Our drawings are designed for your specific site conditions.
