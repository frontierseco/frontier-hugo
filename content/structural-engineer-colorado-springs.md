## Our Services in Colorado Springs

### Commercial Structural Design
New office buildings, retail centers, mixed-use developments, and tenant improvements. We design steel, concrete, masonry, and wood structural systems sized for Colorado's wind and snow loads.

### Residential Structural Engineering
Home additions, deck permits, garage conversions, second-story additions, vaulted ceiling framing, and structural letters for building departments. We work directly with homeowners and their contractors.

### Forensic Structural Engineering
Foundation distress, wall cracking, structural failures, and pre-purchase evaluations. We investigate the cause, document the findings, and give you a clear written report.

### Expert Witness
Licensed PE testimony and expert reports for construction litigation, insurance disputes, and arbitration in El Paso County and throughout Colorado.

### Construction Administration
Submittal review, RFI responses, and site visits during construction to ensure your project is built as designed.

### Peer Review
Independent review of structural drawings and calculations for building departments, owners, and lenders.
