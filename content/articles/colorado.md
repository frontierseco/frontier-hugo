Colorado is one of the fastest-growing states in the country. From the Denver metro to mountain communities, demand for new facilities is accelerating faster than at any point in recent memory: activity centers, mixed-use destinations, commercial hubs. Frontier is built to meet that demand.

## Designing for Colorado's Unique Conditions

Engineering in Colorado isn't the same as engineering anywhere else. The Front Range presents a particular combination of challenges that require local knowledge and experience: high-wind uplift from chinook events, seismic considerations along the Rampart Range fault system, expansive soils in many of the most active development corridors, and the snow loading requirements that come with proximity to the mountains.

We design with these realities baked in from day one, not retrofitted at the end of the process.

## Built for Community

The facilities we design serve people. Activity centers, gyms, food halls, multi-use retail and residential buildings, event venues: these are the spaces where communities gather. Getting the structure right means those spaces can be what they need to be: open, inviting, safe, and enduring.

Our work in Colorado spans new commercial buildings, adaptive retrofits of existing structures, tenant improvements, and everything in between. We're familiar with local jurisdictions and their submittal requirements, which means fewer surprises and faster approvals.

That focus means our Colorado clients get an engineer who is invested in the outcome, responsive to the schedule, and precise in the drawings.
