In today's architectural landscape, creating buildings with expansive glass facades and unobstructed views is a hallmark of luxury and innovation.

At Frontier Structural Engineering, we specialize in moment frame design — a structural system that eliminates the need for diagonal bracing or shear walls, freeing up walls for floor-to-ceiling glass and open floor plans.

Moment frames transfer loads through rigid beam-column connections, allowing architects the freedom to design bold, open spaces without compromising structural integrity.
