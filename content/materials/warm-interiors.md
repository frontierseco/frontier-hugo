## Timber for Warm, Expressive Interiors

There is something fundamentally human about wood. Exposed timber beams, columns, and decking create an atmosphere that no other structural material quite replicates — warm, natural, and grounded. As architects and clients increasingly seek biophilic design elements, structural timber has moved from hidden utility to celebrated feature.

Heavy timber construction — posts, beams, and planks — has a long history in North American building. Today it is being rediscovered and refined, with engineered lumber expanding the spans and load capacities available to designers.

### Structural Expression

When timber is left exposed, the structure becomes the architecture. This demands precise engineering — connections must be both strong and clean, and member sizing must balance structural need with visual proportion.

At Frontier Structural Engineering, we enjoy the challenge of designing timber systems where engineering and aesthetics are inseparable. From custom moment connections to decorative column caps, we help bring warm, structurally honest interiors to life.
