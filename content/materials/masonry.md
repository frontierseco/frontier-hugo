## Masonry Construction

Masonry is one of the oldest and most reliable structural systems in the built environment. Whether brick, concrete masonry unit (CMU), or stone, masonry construction offers exceptional durability, fire resistance, and thermal mass: qualities that continue to make it a strong choice for commercial and residential projects alike.

At Frontier Structural Engineering, we design masonry structures to meet modern code requirements while honoring the inherent strengths of the material. Proper detailing (including wall ties, lintels, bond beams, and reinforcement) ensures long-term performance under lateral and gravity loads.

### Key Advantages

- Long service life with minimal maintenance
- Excellent fire and sound resistance
- High thermal mass for energy efficiency
- Aesthetic versatility: brick, block, stone, and more

### Engineering Considerations

Masonry requires careful attention to reinforcing, especially in seismic zones. We evaluate bearing capacity, lateral resistance, and connection details to ensure every masonry system performs as intended throughout the life of the structure.
