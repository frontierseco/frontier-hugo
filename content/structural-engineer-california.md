## Services Available in California

### Industrial and Energy Facility Structural Design
Equipment foundations, turbine pads, pipe racks, steel platforms, and related infrastructure. We are experienced with the coordination demands of industrial projects and the California Building Code's seismic requirements.

### Commercial Structural Engineering
New construction and tenant improvements for California commercial projects. We work with California's higher seismic design categories and the specific requirements of CBC and ASCE 7.

### Forensic Structural Engineering
Distress investigation, failure analysis, and pre-purchase evaluations for California properties. We provide clear written reports suitable for insurance claims, litigation, and building department submittals.

### Expert Witness
Licensed California PE expert testimony and technical reports for construction disputes and litigation.
